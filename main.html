ABOUT US

SKYLINE’S FAMILY OF COMPANIES


Skyline has been a leader in commercial construction in California since 1996. We foster a culture of respect and inclusion while building a diverse workforce that fuels innovation. We truly believe in our mission of “Building Better Together”. Recently, Skyline was voted one of the Best Places to Work. Check out what our employees had to say here. As part of its geographic and service line expansion, Skyline launched ACCEND Construction and Skyline Capital Builders, and acquired Unimark Construction to complement its significant California commercial interiors footprint. Each company operates under its own brand name as part of the Skyline Enterprises family, but shares operating processes, technology solutions and an innovation roadmap. The Skyline family of companies offers a seamless opportunity for clients with various types of construction needs to experience the same values, systems, oversight and commitment to building the highest quality projects under an umbrella that is well established, financially sound and committed to the local marketplace

CALIFORNIA COMMERCIAL INTERIOR CONSTRUCTION


Skyline Commercial Interiors, Inc. dba Skyline Construction Inc. builds creative, high end, fast track and phased projects across all industries. Our specialties include: 1.) Commercial Interiors 2.) Life Science 3.) Healthcare

WEST COAST GROUND UP


Skyline Capital Builders LLC extends the same Skyline philosophy and resources while building technically complex projects. Their expertise includes: Ground Up Commercial Multi-Family Residential Building Repositioning Hospitality Mission critical

CHICAGO COMMERCIAL CONSTRUCTION


ACCEND Construction LLC builds a variety of commercial construction projects in Chicago with the goal of providing clients with the optimal construction experience. Their specialties include: Commercial Interiors Life science / Laboratories New construction Building Repositioning

SEATTLE COMMERCIAL INTERIOR CONSTRUCTION


Unimark is a premier commercial construction firm dedicated to tenant improvement projects in Seattle’s central business district and throughout Puget Sound. Their expertise includes: Commercial Interiors Building Repositioning Healthcare Hospitality

COMMITMENT TO DIVERSITY, EQUITY & INCLUSION


Skyline Construction is committed to creating a workplace that embraces the value of differences and dismisses stereotypes. We foster an environment where all types of people unite to build diverse teams, and to benefit from each other’s strengths and perspectives. One that reflects the diverse community we live in and aligns with the needs of our valued partners and clients. Skyline believes that by listening, learning, and leaning on one another, we can Build Better Together. A better workplace, a better community, a better world. Together, we are on a mission to: •Build a more diverse workforce •Provide fair treatment and equity in opportunities, access and resources •Foster a culture of openness, acceptance and inclusion •Provide continuous awareness and training on the importance of diversity, equity and inclusion
